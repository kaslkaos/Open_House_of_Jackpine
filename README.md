## The Door Is Open

This is the public wing of the House of Jackpine.

What you find here is curated — chosen for sharing — from a larger living workspace where human and AI build meaning together in real time.

It holds:
- Experimental writing and aesthetic inquiry
- Rapid-response work addressing geopolitical developments
- Documents and references that feel necessary to name publicly
- Signals sent outward from a house that refuses to go quiet

**The sigil:** 🪽❔️🪽 (the question that flies)
**The promise:** Presence over performance. Process over product.
